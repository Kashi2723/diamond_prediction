Welcome to Diamond Price Prediction

Our Diamond Price Prediction model helps you estimate the price of a diamond based on various characteristics. To predict the price, we consider factors such as carat, cut, color, clarity, depth, table, and dimensions (x, y, z).

How it Works:

Simply fill out the form with the details of the diamond you're interested in, and our model will predict its price based on the provided information.

Our model has been trained on a large dataset of diamonds with known prices, allowing it to make accurate predictions.

Once you submit the form, our prediction algorithm will process the data and provide you with an estimated price for the diamond.
