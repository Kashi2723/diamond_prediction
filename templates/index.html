About the Project

Welcome to the revolutionary Diamond Price Prediction project! Our state-of-the-art predictive model harnesses the power of machine learning to estimate the price of diamonds with unparalleled accuracy. Powered by cutting-edge algorithms and a comprehensive dataset, our model analyzes various attributes of diamonds to deliver precise predictions.

Diamond Features

  • Carat: The weight of the diamond, measured in metric carats.
  • Cut: The quality of the diamond's cut, which influences its brilliance and sparkle.
  • Color: The color grade of the diamond, ranging from J (worst) to D (best).
  • Clarity: The presence of internal and external flaws in the diamond.
  • Depth: The height of the diamond from culet to table, expressed as a percentage of mean(x, y).
  • Table: The width of the top facet relative to the widest point.
  • Dimensions (x, y, z): The length, width, and depth of the diamond.

How it Works

These features play a crucial role in determining the price of a diamond. By analyzing these attributes, our predictive model learns patterns and relationships to make accurate price predictions.

To get started, you can input the characteristics of a diamond into the model, and it will provide you with an estimated price based on the dataset it has been trained on.
